Main Mechanics

  • Eating burger gives 40 energy but costs 25 coins.
  • Eating from snack stall 1 gives 30 energy but costs 20 coins.
  • Eating from snack stall 2 gives 28 energy but costs 20 coins.
  • Eating snack bar from green stall gives 25 energy but costs 15 coins.
  • Eating snack bar from red stall gives 24 energy but costs 15 coins.
  • Eating snack bar from street stall gives 24 energy but costs 15 coins.
  • Drinking water gives 20 energy but costs 5 coins.
  • Drinking other drinks give 15 energy but cost 8 coins each.
  • Game machine 1 (Number guessing game) gives 15 coins when player wins.
  • Game machine 3 (Flappy bird game) gives 10 coins when the final score is greater than 1500.
  • Game machine 6 (Chrome dino game) gives 25 coins when the final score is greater than 5000.
  • Game machine crawl gives 10 coins if the final time remaining is greater than 30 seconds.
